## American Roulette Rules and Betting Methods

### Rules Overview

American Roulette features a wheel with 38 slots, numbered 0-36 and an additional 00. The objective is to predict where the ball will land after the wheel is spun. Players can place bets on individual numbers, groups of numbers, colors, or whether the outcome will be odd or even.

### Betting Methods

**Inside Bets:**
- **Straight-up:** Bet on a single number. Example: Bet on '7'.
- **Split Bet:** Bet on two adjacent numbers. Example: Bet on '5' and '6'.
- **Street Bet:** Bet on three numbers in a row. Example: Bet on '1, 2, 3'.
- **Corner Bet:** Bet on four numbers that form a square. Example: Bet on '8, 9, 11, 12'.
- **Five Bet:** Specific to American Roulette, covering 0, 00, 1, 2, and 3.

**Outside Bets:**
- **Red/Black:** Bet on the color of the winning slot.
- **Odd/Even:** Bet on whether the winning number will be odd or even.
- **High/Low:** Bet on whether the winning number will be in the lower range (1-18) or higher range (19-36).
- **Dozens:** Bet on a set of twelve numbers. Example: Bet on '1-12'.
