## Roulette Rules and Variations

Roulette is available in several variations, with the European, American, and French styles being the most prominent:

- **European Roulette**: This variant is preferred by many players, including those in Japan, due to its single zero (0) wheel, which offers a house edge of 2.70%. It is easily accessible in Japanese online casinos.
  
- **American Roulette**: Featuring both a single zero (0) and a double zero (00), American Roulette has a higher house edge of 5.26%. Its popularity is lower among Japanese players due to the increased difficulty in winning.

- **French Roulette**: Similar to European Roulette but includes special rules like "La Partage" and "En Prison," which can further lower the house edge to 1.35% on even-money bets, making it an attractive option for strategic players.

## Bet Types and Payouts

Roulette offers various betting options, each with different odds and payouts:

- **Inside Bets**:
  - **Straight-up**: Bet on a single number; a 35:1 payout.

  - **Street**: Bet on three numbers in a row; an 11:1 payout.
  - **Corner**: Bet on four numbers forming a square; an 8:1 payout.
  - **Six Line**: Bet on two adjacent lines; a 5:1 payout.

  - **Red/Black, Odd/Even, 1-18/19-36**: Offer even money (1:1) payouts.
