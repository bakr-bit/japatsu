## European Roulette Rules and Comparison

### Rules and Bet Types
European Roulette is a well-structured table game featuring a wheel with 37 pockets numbered from 0 to 36. Key differences from the American variant include a single zero (0) which improves odds for players. The primary bet types are:

- **Inside Bets:** Placed directly on the numbers, including bets on single numbers, splits (two adjacent numbers), streets (three numbers in a row), and corners (four numbers).
- **Outside Bets:** Placed on categories such as red or black, odd or even, and high or low numbers. These offer lower payouts but higher chances of winning.

### RTP and House Edge
European Roulette offers a Return to Player (RTP) rate of approximately 97.3%, due to its lower house edge of 2.7% when compared to American Roulette, which has an RTP of 94.74% owing to its double zero (00).
