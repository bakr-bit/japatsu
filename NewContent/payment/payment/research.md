Cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum are gaining acceptance for their anonymity and low transaction fees. Direct bank transfers, while slower, are used for their perceived security. Fees are generally higher, and processing times can extend to several days, affecting user experience negatively in terms of immediacy. This method often appeals to users who prioritize security over speed.

### Emerging Methods
New trends include the use of QR code-based payments linked to traditional bank accounts, offering a blend of security and convenience. These systems are tailored to the digital banking habits of younger users in Japan.

Each payment method varies in terms of deposit and withdrawal processes.

- **Credit Cards**: Immediate deposits with potential withdrawal delays due to bank processing times. Limits are generally moderate, with fees depending on the casino.

- **Bank Transfers**: Slowest option with high fees and extended processing times. Typically used by those prioritizing security.
