## KYC and Verification Procedures

Casinos require players to undergo Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures as part of compliance with anti-money laundering laws in Japan. This includes verifying identity through government-issued identification and sometimes proof of address.

## Reliability and Security

Bank transfers are regarded as reliable and secure. They mitigate the risk of fraud by requiring dual verification through bank interfaces. Dispute resolution mechanisms are typically robust, allowing players to contact their bank or the casino for issues. However, chargebacks are usually more challenging compared to credit card transactions, thus necessitating caution.

## Comparison with Alternative Payment Methods

- **Credit Cards**: Offers quick transactions but may incur higher fees and have stricter regulations.
- **E-Wallets**: Provide speed and convenience but may not be as universally accepted.
- **Cryptocurrencies**: Offer anonymity and lower fees but carry volatility and limited acceptance.

Recent legislative enhancements focus on increasing digital transaction security and safeguarding against financial crimes.
