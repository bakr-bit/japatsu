### Rules and Gameplay
Three Card Poker consists of two primary betting options: the Ante/Play and the Pair Plus. Players compete directly against the dealer using three cards each.

- **Ante/Play Bet**: The player starts by placing an Ante bet. After viewing their cards, they can choose to either fold, forfeiting the Ante, or continue with a Play bet, equal to the Ante. The dealer must have at least a Queen high to qualify. If the dealer does not qualify, the Ante bet is paid out even money, and the Play bet pushes.
  
- **Pair Plus Bet**: This is a wager on the player's hand containing a pair or better. The outcome of this bet is independent of the dealer's hand.
