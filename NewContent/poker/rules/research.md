## Gameplay Phases

### Texas Hold'em Breakdown

1. **Preflop**: Players receive two hole cards. Betting starts.
2. **Flop**: Three community cards are revealed. Another round of betting follows.
3. **Turn**: A fourth community card is revealed. Betting continues.
4. **River**: The fifth and final community card is revealed. Final round of betting.
5. **Showdown**: Remaining players reveal their cards to determine the winner.

### Three Card Poker

In Three Card Poker, players aim to have a higher-ranking three-card hand than the dealer. The steps include placing an ante, receiving cards, deciding to fold or raise, and possibly qualifying for bonus payouts depending on the hand.

### Caribbean Stud Poker

This variant involves players competing against the dealer. Players start with an ante, receive five cards, and can fold or raise after seeing the dealer's reveal. Payouts depend on both player's hand strength and the dealer's ability to qualify.
